二、5–10分钟小测

1. A brief and clear explanation is ____.

  1. convoluted
  2. concise
  3. contentious
  4. credulous
显示答案

B. concise

2. To confirm a claim with evidence is to ____ it.

  1. corroborate
  2. condone
  3. construe
  4. concede
显示答案

A. corroborate

3. A person who believes too easily is ____.

  1. cosmopolitan
  2. conscientious
  3. credulous
  4. contrite
显示答案

C. credulous

4. A topic likely to cause argument is ____.

  1. conciliatory
  2. contentious
  3. concurrent
  4. conspicuous
显示答案

B. contentious

5. General agreement among a group is called ____.

  1. consensus
  2. contention
  3. consternation
  4. conundrum
显示答案

A. consensus

6. A calm, peace-making tone is ____.

  1. condescending
  2. conciliatory
  3. contemptuous
  4. conservative
显示答案

B. conciliatory

7. Something obvious and easily seen is ____.

  1. conspicuous
  2. consummate
  3. contrived
  4. copious
显示答案

A. conspicuous

8. A difficult puzzle or problem is a ____.

  1. connoisseur
  2. conundrum
  3. contention
  4. consensus
显示答案

B. conundrum
