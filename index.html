204. circumscribed

IPA 音标：/ˈsɝː.kəm.skraɪbd/

拼读：SER-kum-skrybd

中文：有界限的；受限制的

英文：marked off, bounded

例句发音：

SAT例句：
The committee worked within a circumscribed budget and could not fund every project.

中文翻译：
委员会在有限的预算内工作，无法资助每一个项目。

💡 SAT重点词汇老师讲解

circum- 表示 around，scribe 和画线有关；circumscribed 就像被画了一圈边界，表示范围受限。

205. circumspect

IPA 音标：/ˈsɝː.kəm.spekt/

拼读：SER-kum-spekt

中文：谨慎的；小心的

英文：cautious

例句发音：

SAT例句：
A circumspect leader considers every risk before making a decision.

中文翻译：
谨慎的领导者在做决定前会考虑每一种风险。

💡 SAT重点词汇老师讲解

SAT高频态度词，常和 cautious, prudent 同义。不是害怕，而是深思熟虑。

206. circumvent

IPA 音标：/ˌsɝː.kəmˈvent/

拼读：ser-kum-VENT

中文：规避；绕开

英文：to get around

例句发音：

SAT例句：

中文翻译：
这家公司试图利用法律漏洞规避规定。

💡 SAT重点词汇老师讲解

circum- around + vent 去；核心是“绕过去”，常用于法律、规则、限制。

Day 8 SAT Reading｜A Small Town Investigation

A Small Town Investigation The town council was usually coherent and calm, but one morning a clamor erupted outside city hall. Residents had learned about a clandestine plan to build a colossus of a shopping center on the last green field in town. The mayor tried to circumvent public discussion, but a circumspect editor named Mira remained cognizant of the risks. Mira wrote a cogent article explaining that the project’s benefits were not commensurate with its damage. She noted that the field was circumscribed by old stone walls and had long served as a place where families could gather. Several neighborhood groups decided to coalesce into one larger organization. The developer offered collateral to secure loans and promised a commodious community room, but many residents feared complacency. They did not want sweet, cloying promises to distract them from the truth. In the end, Mira’s compelling report helped the town protect the field and create a public garden instead.

Main Idea 主旨句题

Q. Which sentence best states the main idea of the story?

点击显示答案

Careful thinking and a compelling argument can help a community resist secret plans and protect what matters.

  2. Q2. Why is Mira described as circumspect?
    点击显示答案

    Because she carefully considers the risks and facts before responding.

  3. Q3. Which word in the story means “to avoid or get around”?
    点击显示答案

  4. Q4. Why did the neighborhood groups coalesce?
    点击显示答案

  5. Q5. What does the story suggest about complacency?
