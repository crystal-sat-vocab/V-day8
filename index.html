二、5–10分钟小测

1. A leader who acts with great caution and care may be described as ____.

  1. commodious
  2. circumspect
  3. colloquial
  4. cogent
显示答案

B. circumspect

2. A secret plan carried out privately can be called ____.

  1. clandestine
  2. cloying
  3. coherent
  4. commensurate
显示答案

A. clandestine

3. If two groups join together into one larger unit, they ____.

  1. coalesce
  2. coerce
  3. coagulate
  4. compensate
显示答案

A. coalesce

4. An argument that is especially convincing is ____.

  1. cognizant
  2. cogent
  3. collateral
  4. complacent
显示答案

B. cogent

5. A student who is fully aware of the risk is ____ of the consequences.

  1. collusive
  2. cognizant
  3. clairvoyant
  4. circumscribed
显示答案

B. cognizant

6. A room with a lot of space can be described as ____.

  1. commodious
  2. combustible
  3. commensurate
  4. cloying
显示答案

A. commodious

7. If two people secretly work together to cheat, that is ____.

  1. collusion
  2. commendation
  3. clemency
  4. clamor
显示答案

A. collusion

8. A speech that strongly holds the audience’s attention is ____.

  1. complacent
  2. compelling
  3. circumspect
  4. coagulated
显示答案

B. compelling

四、阅读找词任务

During the investigation, the committee remained circumspect, refusing to release details before the facts were clear. Even so, a clamor grew outside the courthouse as reporters demanded updates. One journalist claimed to be clairvoyant about the outcome, but her dramatic prediction was less cogent than the careful report written by a more cognizant editor. Meanwhile, two small neighborhood groups chose to coalesce into one organization so they could more effectively oppose the developer’s clandestine plan.

1. Which word in the passage means “careful and cautious”?

显示答案

circumspect

2. Which word means “a loud public outcry or noise”?

显示答案

clamor

3. Which word means “having unusual powers of perception”?

显示答案

clairvoyant

4. Which word means “convincing”?

显示答案

cogent

5. Which word means “aware”?

显示答案

cognizant

6. Which word means “join together into one whole”?

显示答案

coalesce

7. Which word means “secret”?

显示答案

clandestine
